ASM Tools

Independent tools for ASM buyers and practitioners — from shortlisting and vendor evaluation through program assessment and procurement. Interactive use is free. Export requires a premium subscription.

Use the vendor comparison tool to filter and shortlist before evaluating. Use the evaluation checklist once you have a shortlist and need a structured way to score two platforms head-to-head.

  • Shortlisting Free
    Vendor Comparison Tool
    Filter the full ASM vendor landscape by architectural cluster, ASM type, discovery method, CTEM alignment, and pricing. No sponsored placements — vendors appear based on fit, not commercial relationships.

  • Vendor evaluation Export: Premium
    EASM Platform Evaluation Checklist
    Score two platforms across 21 criteria covering discovery, validation, prioritization, mobilization, CTEM alignment, and procurement. Select from known vendors or score any platform. Export a PDF report ready to share with your team.

    Per-Asset Pricing Calculator
    Estimate what a per-asset daily pricing model will actually cost once the platform discovers your full attack surface — not just what's in your inventory today. Enter your environment profile to see the gap between budgeted and likely actual cost.
